Hour of AI

Making Artificial Intelligence Accessible Through Education

Overview

Hour of AI is an educational initiative designed to make artificial intelligence easy to understand and accessible for a wide audience. This ongoing project is being developed by Team #15, consisting of four members, with the goal of providing an engaging and beginner-friendly introduction to AI concepts.

The project aims to break down complex AI topics into simple explanations, focusing on their real-world applications instead of technical complexity.

Current Status

The project is in progress, with content development, structure, and presentation materials being actively worked on. The project is scheduled for completion by December 20.

Project Features

  • Introductory explanations of AI concepts
  • Interactive and visual learning tools
  • Team-based presentations
  • Certification upon successful completion
